Sean Sear's How to Plan to Write Your News Story

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

Introduction

Assembly time: 2 hours
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Have you ever wondered how to plan for writing a news story? On this page are the 6 most important steps that go behind planning your news story/article.

  • Things You'll Need

  • Access to Computer and Internet
  • Notepad and writing utensil
  • Article Outline
  • Access to communication services
  • People willing to be interviewed

  1. Research Your Topic

    To begin writing a news article you need to research the topic you will be writing about extensively. In order to succeed in creating a well written news article, you must have plentiful information on the topic, as well as resources. There are 6 W's: Who was involved, what happened, where did it happen, when did it happen, how did it happen?

  2. Compile All of Your Facts

    Jot down a list of all the facts and information that needs to be included in the article. Organize your facts into three groups: Those needed in the article, those that are interesting but not needed, and those that are related but not important.

    Make sure to jot down all of your facts!

  3. Create an Article Outline

    Creating an outline is an important step to lead you into writing a good news article. Start with your more important information at the top, and be sure to place emphasis in this outline.

  4. Know Your Audience

    It is very important to know your audience when writing a news story. Knowing who you are writing to will help create a certain mood and tone when writing the news article.

  5. Find an Angle.

    Try your best to find a unique angle. If you are writing about a popular news story, it is especially important to be creative. Having your own, different view on a subject can really help your news article.

  6. Interview People

    It is a great benefit to get a first hand source into your news article. You will become much mroe reliable, and it is more intriguing for your audience to hear from a first hand source> This will make your news article to be looked at as more authentic.
